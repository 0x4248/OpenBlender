What is open blender?

OpenBlender is a collection of free and open-source assets for Blender. These assets are free to use and modify in any project and are licensed under the CC-0 license.

What is CC-0?

CC-0 is a public domain license that allows creators to waive all their copyright and related rights. This means that you can use, modify, and distribute the assets in any way you want without having to worry about licensing or attribution.
